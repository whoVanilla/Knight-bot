Colors - 
Red (error) = #fc0303
Orange (warn) = #ff8400
Green (success) = #53c26d
Black (general) = #000000
White (general) = #ffffff
Purple (information) = #ab7aff

Console color codes - 
Bright Red: \x1b[91m
Bright Green: \x1b[92m
Bright Yellow: \x1b[93m
Bright Cyan: \x1b[96m
Bright White: \x1b[97m
Reset: \x1b[0m
Bold: \x1b[1m
